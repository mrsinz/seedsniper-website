Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2024

1. Information We Collect

SeedSniper may collect the following information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App Usage: Features used, preferences, notification settings
  • Push Notification Tokens: For Pro users who enable notifications
  • Subscription Status: Pro membership status and subscription details

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide app functionality and features
  • Send push notifications for stock updates (Pro users only)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Process subscription purchases through Apple App Store

3. Data Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Apple: For App Store functionality and In-App Purchases
  • Google AdMob: For advertising services
  • Google Sheets: For Pro user notification token storage

4. Push Notifications

Pro users who enable notifications:

  • Device tokens are stored securely for notification delivery
  • Notifications are sent based on user preferences and rarity filters
  • Users can disable notifications anytime in Settings

5. Advertising

SeedSniper uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information for personalized advertising. Users can opt out of personalized ads in their device settings.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All data transmission is encrypted.

7. Third-Party Services

SeedSniper integrates with:

  • Grow A Garden API (for stock data)
  • Discord (for community support)
  • Apple App Store (for purchases)
  • Google AdMob (for advertising)

8. Children's Privacy

SeedSniper is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns:

  • App: SeedSniper Settings → Support Discord
  • Discord: https://discord.gg/6BqKN7NtZj
